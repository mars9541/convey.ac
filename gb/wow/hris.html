Big Budget Buyers

Its true that being in the right place at the right time is the best way to maximise the chance of a sale but you also need to have what the buyer is looking to buy otherwise you wont meet the requirements to secure a meeting.

Big budget buyers have long buying cycles which involve multiple people with constantly evolving checklists so you need to ensure you have what they are looking for, after all the lead buyers will need to justify their purchasing decisions and show how they have taken into account not only their companies current needs but also the companies future needs as well. Having what buyers want now may get you shortlisted, but in a highly competitive business like this you need to have what buyers are planning to use in the future to really close the sale.

So, what is it you should be factoring in that you may have overlooked, well, many larger businesses are now looking to upgrade from software that creates static records (records stored locally on isolated databases) to software that creates conveyable records (records that are copied to a central server so they can CONVEY from one employer to the next). Future proofing your software now by building conveyability into your functionality will enable you to show active buyers that you are already aligned with their long term needs making it easier for them to commit to a purchase.

User Churn Rate

Its twice as hard to aquire new users if you're know for having a high churn rate.

Many software providers make the mistake of overestimating their user loyalty and falsely presume they can leverage some level of vendor lockin to stabilize their churn rate. Whilst all users like to stay with whats familiar, as soon as they have a functionality requirement that their existing provider no longer fulfils they will start looking elsewhere and no level of discounting will reverse that.

Keeping ahead of user demand is the only real way to cap user churn and protect your brand, every month more and more businesses are choosing to upgrade from static records to conveyable records so building conveyability into your own software now will prove to be a wise defensive measure.

Give Your Users the Choice

Building conveyability into your software is easy, when one of your users creates a record using your software it is entered and stored in your own local database, a simple backend process then sends a copy of that entry to the CONVEY Databank where a duplicate of that record is created, it is that duplicate that becomes conveyable. Your users have to activate the conveyability aspect themselves for privacy reasons meaning some of your users will create conveyable records whilst others will stay with the current static version.

Once you have built conveyability into your software we will add your details to our 'List of Approved Providers'. Any business wanting to create conveyable records using better software than what we provide for free will be able to see your details and visit your site.
